During our complimentary consultation, we embark on a thorough exploration of your company, objectives, and vision. This in-depth understanding serves as the foundation for crafting a bespoke and highly personalized proposal tailored specifically to your unique needs and aspirations.

Proposal Creation

Harnessing insights from your contact form and our detailed consultation, our proposal becomes the roadmap for success. It covers website development, marketing strategies, performance tracking, and future adaptability. This concise guide ensures each step is purposeful, aligning your goals with our strategic approach.

Confirmation

During the confirmation phase, it's decision time. We'll discuss the prospect of moving forward together or exploring alternative paths. This is the moment to dive into finer details, iron out collaboration specifics, and ensure that our working relationship aligns seamlessly. Whether it's a green light to proceed or a pause for adjustments, this phase sets the tone for a productive partnership.

Contract

Once the plan aligns with your satisfaction, we'll proceed to formalize the agreement. This involves the completion and signing of formal contracts. This step solidifies our commitment and ensures clarity on expectations, delivering a secure foundation for the journey ahead.

Development & Quality Assurance

Plan approved? Let the magic begin. Our development cycle kicks into high gear, crafting your vision into reality. We test, tweak, and ensure every detail aligns with the strategy. It's not just about a website; it's about creating a digital experience that exceeds expectations. Expect open communication as we bring your project to life.

Google Tag Manager

Next on the agenda is Google Tag Manager implementation, seamlessly aligning with our tracking plan. This strategic move ensures precision in monitoring and analyzing user interactions on your website. By efficiently managing tags and tracking codes, we enhance the accuracy of data collection, providing invaluable insights into user behavior. It's a step that propels your digital presence to a new level of informed decision-making.

Google Analytics 4

From Google Tag Manager, we smoothly move on to setting up Google Analytics 4. This step turns collected data into actionable insights, providing a deeper understanding of user behavior and improving overall website performance. It's data-driven decision-making in action.

Client training & videos

Training time! We'll provide short, insightful videos to empower you in managing your digital space. Plus, we're all ears for your requests. Before the big moment, it's time for the final review. We're ensuring every detail is buttoned up, from the website's functionality to the Google Ads strategy. It's the last check to guarantee we're set for the go-live stage. This thorough review ensures nothing slips through the cracks, and your digital presence is primed and ready for success. Let's make sure we've got all bases covered!
